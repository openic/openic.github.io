    here are the steps..

    1. Step 1

      First of all go to top plane and sketch a circle.

    2. Step 2

      Create an offset plane of 120mm.

    3. Step 3

      create another circle.

    4. Step 4

      then go and select the loft command.

    5. Step 5

      Select the 2 profiles and click on preview and then click on the third button on the profile select buttons.

    6. Step 6

      Select normal to end 1

    7. Step 7

      Thin wall..

    8. Step 8
