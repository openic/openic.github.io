Flute ROCK Vapor concept.

The first thing i want to say, is this rock vapor concept was inspired in all the world of rock, have a cool style look, and functions that makes the rock vapor become better than ever, in the functionality we have a very simple read display who save battery because every time you adjust your settings the display brights on for just of 10 seconds and then starts automatically ECO consumption mode and after 10 seconds turn off the display , the buttons are aesthetically distributed to looks and feel with the simple touch and also inspired in a flute thats why the concept takes a lot of the conception of the solo flute in the song stairway to heaven by Led Zeppelin, for another hand other of the big inspiration in this design comes with the people who enjoy attending concerts, and support our favorite band, the part in the rock button have double functionality, first if you press and hold you can puffing, but when its time to support your favorite rock band then press 2 times and the e-liquid turns on.

I call this "the lighter mode" its time to put your hand in the air and support your favorite rockband.

Also we have an ergonomic piece mouth, which makes confortable and easy the inhale.

We have a battery rechargable and 3 little leds, this makes more easy and fast the identification of the full charge, also this help to avoid overcharging the device, and how is low consumption you have all the time the status of the battery and no need turn on the blue screen at least you need aditional functions or adjust settings helping to save power for the puffing and "rocking lighter mode".

The dimensions are 170mm x 17mm x 17mm.

Thanks for watch my job.
