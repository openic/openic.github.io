Same design as my first mop, but with a triangular head. The handle extends using two spring loaded buttons on the right and left sides, such as on most pool skimmers, and the head is attached using a pivot joint. The cloth attaches to the head via three hooks on top of the head that the cloth would be stretched over. It will be made out of plastic except for the joint, which will be metal.
