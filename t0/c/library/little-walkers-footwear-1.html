These shoes are based on the disturbing numbers of children without shoes in poverty stricken countries across the world.

No child's first steps should be full of pain and discomfort.

The shoes is very basic. It has a simple strap allowing for a very young age to just slip their feet in. The sole of the shoe is fairly thick, with deep tread due to the rough conditions of the grounds they walk upon.

These could come in a variety of colours, all depending on the printers capability.

For very small feet to stay within the competition rules.

They could be produced for larger feet but this would use much more than 52 grams of plastic.
