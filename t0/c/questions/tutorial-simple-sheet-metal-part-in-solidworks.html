Tutorial - Simple sheet metal part in SolidWorks?

SOLIDWORKS solidworks part metal sheet simple tutorial
By Sudhir Gill on 03 Feb 16:42 3 answers 3850 views 3 comments

Tutorial on sheet metal in Solidworks.

3 answers

  • Sudhir Gill over 2 years ago

    Here is the Tutorial.

    1. Step 1

      Start Solidworks in part mode.

    2. Step 2

      Top Plane>>Sketch.

    3. Step 3

      Make a rectangle.

    4. Step 4

      Under Sheetmetal tab choose base flange. This will act as the base of the sheetmetal part.

    5. Step 5

      Keep the default settings and click OK.

    6. Step 6

      The base of the sheetmetal part has been generated.

    7. Step 7

      Choose Edge flange in sheetmetal tab.

    8. Step 8

      Choose the right face lower edge.

    9. Step 9

      Enter a height of 70mm in Property manager.

    10. Step 10

      Click OK and we have the wall created.

    11. Step 11

      Repeat the same step for the other edges also.

    12. Step 12

      Again Select the edge flange and select the edge of the middle wall.

    13. Step 13

      Enter an height of 150mm and Click OK.

    14. Step 14

      Open Design Library.

    15. Step 15

      Browse to Forming Tools>>Louvers. If needed right click the forming tools folder and click the forming tool option.

    16. Step 16

      Drag the lower to the top surface of the part.

    17. Step 17

      Position it by assigning some dimensions.

    18. Step 18

      Click OK and we have the forming feature created.

    19. Step 19

      Choose Edge flange and select the Edge of the bottom surface.

    20. Step 20

      Click OK.

    21. Step 21

      Choose Flatten in the Sheetmetal Tab. And we have the sheetmetal body part created.
