Over the past two years I've dreamed of purchasing a BOXX system, and I was most attracted by their PC's ability to run Solidworks. For this GrabCAD'r, it would make the world of difference going forward in 2014.

But hey! Now it's everyone's time to dream!

This design features interlocking gears on each side of the case, with one side a transparent window to the beauty inside. Based from the existing 4150 BOXX system, I reincarnated it to have CAD in mind...and body ;)

This new design also has all the expandability of the current BOXX station, including most of the architecture inside-and-out.

This workstation from a distance will be recognized for producing serious CAD solutions!!

Hope you like!
