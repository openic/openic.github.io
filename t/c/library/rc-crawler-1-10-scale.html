This is a RC Crawler I designed from pictures and only knowing the specs of a class 2.2 crawler and size of rims tires motor servo and battery.
I never intended to build it, just made it for fun.
I later bought the the one it is base on.
It did no survived well the conversion from SW 2007 to 2010 hence the missing parts(differential and transmission gears). But the model was complete at first.
I wanted to share so I extracted a Step file from what I have left.
I didn't knew a lot about RCs back then, today I would change some of its parts specialy the transmission.
