An RC plane propeller which can be used by various people like students, scientists, engineers, toy makers etc. to make RC airplanes. Children of rural areas can be taught about Aviation and Aeromodelling using such parts(by 3D printing) where it is hard to find them in faraway places.
A part which can easily be mass produced, has various applications and can easily be made by 1 plastic bottle or maybe even half!
And moreover it can easily be 3D printed too!
