Personal vaporizer inspired by the old fountain pens, giving it an aggressive and modern look.
Aluminum was chosen as the main material for its lightness, strength and quality feel, opting for a rubbed surface finish to ensure a firm grip without neglecting aesthetics.
Though obviously you could always make plastic for a more economical line.

For its functioning it has been decided to use a sensor, wherein each time you inhale the vaporizer is activated, because having to press a button to smoking can be uncomfortable and not very ergonomic (each person grabs it of different way)
The buttons would serve to choose the information that is displayed, as well as to modify some of the parameters .Besides, it has been chosen a USB type-B due to its current standardization on smartphones
