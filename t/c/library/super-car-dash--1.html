This is my proposal for the design of the 500Group Supercar dash.
It draws inspiration from the natural light display in the sky called Aurora Borealis. It transmits beauty, power, movement, energy and mystery. It is also a modern 3D sleek and clean design, that is configured to fully interact with the driver.
I also include a video and some additional classic skins for the driver to change the look of the dash according to his own taste.
Hope you enjoy it!
