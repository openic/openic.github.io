Patterns on a curved surface?

SOLIDWORKS surface pattern curved
By mark on 20 Dec 00:52 3 answers 2910 views 0 comments

How do I put a pattern on a curved surface so that each element of the pattern is parallel to the curved surface? I cant figure it out, would it be a curve driven pattern? Anyone know of any good tutorials for reproducing similar forms? Thanks!!!!!

  • Rohit Mitra over 2 years ago

    The easiest way to do it is to use the holewizard to create your "seed feature" (the pattern that is going to repeat) and then use a linear pattern or a curve driven pattern (depending on the geometry) to fill in the part. Then you can use the holewizard to fill in any gaps. The reason I would use the holewizard is that it allows you to quickly create multiple cuts that remain Normal to the surface no matter where you place the reference point.
