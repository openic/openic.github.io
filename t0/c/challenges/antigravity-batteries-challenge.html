Antigravity Batteries needs a unique, attractive, and efficient battery case design.

Design a new exterior case for the Micro-Start XP-1


Antigravity Batteries seeks to develop a new exterior case design for it's world's smallest jump starter/personal power supply. We are seeking a modern, high tech looking design while taking into consideration relatively simple tooling and production. Black and red are our main brand colors but we are open to black and yellow designs, as well.

World's smallest Portable Jump Starter and Back-up Power Supply

The Micro-Start PPS (Personal Power Supply) is the world's smallest Jump Starter and Personal Power Supply (only 6” x 2-7/8” x 1”). It is so small it fits in your pocket yet so powerful it can start motorcycles and even V8 Trucks!!! It charges and powers most devices: Laptops (not compatible with Apple 16v Laptops), Cell Phones, iPads, iPhones, Kindles, PSPs, PDAs, GPS, MP3s, Blue Tooth Devices, Cameras and more.

Just charge it once and take back-up power whereever you go. It weighs less than a pound (14 oz.) so you can take it with you whether you are riding, driving, camping or boating. Remain confident that you will be able to start your vehicle, make that important call, or get that last bit of work done.

View a demonstration of the Antigravity Batteries Micro-Start XP-1 Jump Starter

Current Product, pictures, other designs as well as files related to the product

What can the Micro-Start do?


  • It jump starts cars, trucks, motorcycles, boats, ATVs, electronics and more.
  • It will jump start your V8 truck several times in a row!
  • It charges and powers most devices: Laptops, Cell Phones, iPads, GPS, and more.
  • It has 3 power ports: 19v, 12v standard output, and 5v USB.
  • It has a high power LED flashlight with 2 built-in flash patterns including an S.O.S. flashing beacon.
  • It has a lighted battery capacity indicator. Press a button to see the battery capacity left in the Micro-Start.
  • It has over-charge and over-discharge protections for long battery life.
  • It has an automatic power-off when not in use.

Requirements

  • You must include:
    - STEP or IGES version
    - Rendering
    - Description of your design

  • You are encouraged to include:
    - STL version
    - Native CAD file in any format
    - More renderings

  • Winning design will be:
    - Attractive
    - Unique
    - Fit Antigravity Batteries brand
    - Thoughtful and efficient

  • Design guidelines:


    • Maximum dimensions: 160mm x 75mm x 28mm
    • Colors: Black + red (preferred)
      Black + yellow (second option)
    • Plan for ports and buttons:
      ...Power button
      ...LED lights for indicator
      ...Jump Start Port with cover or flap
      ...5v USB port
      ...12v port (2.1mm x 5.5 mm Standard DC port)
      ...19v port (2.1 mm x 5.5 mm Standard DC port)
      ...Charging port (3.5mm x 1.1mm standard DC port)
    • Efficient designs will keep the circuit board ports (USB, 12v, 19v, Charging Port Battery) located on one side of the product for easier circuit board design.
    • The case is manufactured via plastic injection.


      Keep in mind, these are preferences and do not mean you shouldn't create something unique and innovative. New ideas are encouraged. The main goal is a great looking case.
