    1. Step 1

      Step 1
      Create a point on the first sketch which is horizontal to the xy plane

    2. Step 2

      Step 2
      Create a point on the second sketch which is horizontal to the yz plane

    3. Step 3

      Step 3
      Click on Multi-Surfaces definition tool in Generative Shape Design Workbench adn add both sketches. Then click with right mouse button on Closing point 1 (not on the arrow) label and pick "Replace". After that just click on the point made in step 1. Repeat for Closing point 2.
