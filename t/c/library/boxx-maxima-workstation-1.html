BOXX MAXIMA is designed to be the compact workstation of tomorrow, it uses Class A surfacing borrowed from automotive design to wrap its internal components.

Chassis is built in stamped steel and the panels hydroformed in aluminum. It features all the requirements of the 3DBOXX 4150 EXTREME, plus two front USB 3.0 ports for a total of 14, a hot swap 3.5 inch HDD bay and capacitive ON/OFF/RESET switch.

It also uses PCIe x16 risers in order to accomodate the two full size GPUs vertically, allowing the blowers on them to borrow air directly from the two front 120mm high performance fans, allowing the GPU to stay cool under load and specially in multi GPU configuration.

Both the panels and the front frame are customizable, liquid cooling is included, and also an option to implement a RAID 10 array with two SSDs and two 3.5" HDDs.

The design is very clean, and attempts to capture the attention of the user with it's surface work, not it's trinkets.

Dimensions
Height 400mm
Width 180mm
Depth 436mm

NOTE. Some of the components are imported meshes, so they won't show in the STEP/IGES files.
