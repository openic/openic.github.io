Steintrikes "Mungo" (in US sold as "Meerkat") complete frame assembly, but saved as a part file to avoid potential rebuild errors. Most of the work is mine, I "grabbed" the brake disc, the brake levers and the Shimano cassette from GrabCAD, some of the parts are already available here and I will select the frame parts and upload them separately for those who want to have an assembly file of this trike...
To complete it, you need a crankset with pedals, derailleurs, shifters and some cables and chain...
Hope you’ll like it... (’cause I spent really a lot of time on this...) :)
All comments are welcome and appreciated!
