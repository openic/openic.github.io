Tutorial on Modeling M20 Bolt in SolidEdge.

1 answer

  • Sumit Balhara about 2 years ago

    Tutorial.

    1. Step 1

      Start SolidEdge.

    2. Step 2

      Circle tool and then select origin.

    3. Step 3

      Make a circle of 20mm diameter on top plane by locking it clicking on the lock icon.

    4. Step 4

      Select Extrude.

    5. Step 5

      Select the circle and then right click.

    6. Step 6

      Extrude by 100.

    7. Step 7

      Polygon tool.

    8. Step 8

      Top face and then select the center of the edge.

    9. Step 9

      Distance be 16.5mm and angle 0.

    10. Step 10

      Select the region of the polygon.

    11. Step 11

      While pressing the control key on the keyboard select the region on the circular face also.

    12. Step 12

      Extrude it by 12.5mm.

    13. Step 13

      Select chamfer equal setbacks.

    14. Step 14

      Lower edge and input value 5.

    15. Step 15

      Front view (Control key + F).

    16. Step 16

      Draw a line along Z-axis of 85mm with endpoint at origin.

    17. Step 17

      Polygon by Center.

    18. Step 18

      Click the intersection of these two edges.

    19. Step 19

      Input Distance 1mm.

    20. Step 20

      Select helix under cut features.

    21. Step 21

      Select the line.

    22. Step 22

      Select the polygon.

    23. Step 23

      Click Tick mark button.

    24. Step 24

      Input turns 22.5mm. Right click to accept it.

    25. Step 25

      Now go to the end face of the thread. Select the face and then project the edges.

    26. Step 26

      Extrude it by remove at ~10mm.

    27. Step 27

      Our bolt is ready.
