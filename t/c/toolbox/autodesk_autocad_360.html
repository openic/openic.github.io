Supported file types

  1. AutoCAD (.dwg, .dxf)

Recommended browsers

  1. Chrome 25 or later
  2. Firefox 19 or later
  3. Internet Explorer 10 or later
  4. Safari 5.1 or later (Mac only)

Description

With AutoCAD® 360 on Workbench, you can view, edit, measure, and share CAD drawings anytime, anywhere.

AutoCAD 360 offers:

  • Save changes directly to your private Workbench projects
  • Collaborate on changes in real time with both engineers and non-engineers
  • Access your drawings anytime, anywhere from your Mac or PC

Pricing

Free

All GrabCAD Workbench users can join the free beta.

How to use

  1. Open a DWG or DXF file in your secure Workbench project
  2. View and edit the drawing within Workbench
  3. Save any changes you make for your collaborators to see
