GrabCAD security protects your data

Your designs and files are your most important assets, and we protect them with the highest level of security in the industry.

View the GrabCAD Security Whitepaper

Your trust is a privilege we take very seriously

These are just some of the features of our security approach.

Securely encrypted

All Workbench data use secure https URLs, protected with industry standard AES 256-bit encryption.

Powered by Amazon

We rely on Amazon’s Elastic Compute Cloud (EC2) platform for secure network access and standards-compliant infrastructure.

Industry-leading certifications

All Workbench data is stored in Amazon S3 servers, which are certified compliant with SAS 70 Type II, ISO 27001, and PCI DSS Level 1 among others.

Securely social

We support two factor authentication for those users who wish to access our site via Facebook or Google.

Third party tested

We employ third parties to conduct penetration tests to ensure our network and application are secure against outside attacks.

Fully encrypted data backup

We protect your content by backing it up to encrypted backup media serveral times a day.
