    Just adding a tutorial How to Use soft blend, it gives a Clear Idea

    1. Step 1

      Most Commonly we come across inAccurate Geometry or dead sharp corner Created even after using Edge Blend , this is just One scenario There are Many Other Area we can Explore The Option

    2. Step 2

      Even if try to Add one more Blend Over it Never Gives an Accurate Result

    3. Step 3

      and Looks Ugly and Difficult for Cutter to Run While Programing , it could Have Been better if the Fillet Rolled over the Edge

    4. Step 4

      Here i defines Few lines, and I am Forcing My Blend to Follow the Path Which I want it to Follow, Usually in Edge Blend i will not Have any control over the Edge of the Blend

    5. Step 5

      Starts With selecting First set (First Face)

    6. Step 6

      As usual like Face Blend select second Set (Second Face)

    7. Step 7

      And Defines First Tang ency Curve ( Same side of First Set Face)

    8. Step 8

      And Defines second Tang ency Curve ( Same side of second Set Face)

    9. Step 9

      and Defines Center Spline Curve

    10. Step 10

      The Result Can Be Obtained as , the Blend flows following the Defined Tang ency Curves

    11. Step 11

      Comparison with " Edge Blend " and " Face Blend "

      Conclusion: just like as we can Define the Edge of a surface in Through curve Mesh, we can define the Edge of Blend or in simple words we can Force Blend to Follow the Path we like
