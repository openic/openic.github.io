The main features of the design are:
- 2 adult and 2 children passengers
- 4 wheels
- a single engine for drive wheels and rear rotor
- a huge wingspan
- air intake under the wings
- easily foldable wings (no need to exit the craft)
- nice view from the cabin ;)
The name comes from this little birdie http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nuthatch (I wasn’t trying to be clever with it) :D
This is my final entry, since I won't have much time over the weekend to make another one ;) so I wish the best of luck to everyone who entered it, it sure was an awesome competition
