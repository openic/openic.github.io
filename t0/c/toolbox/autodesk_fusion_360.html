  • Autodesk Fusion 360

  • Autodesk Inc.
  • Publish your Autodesk Fusion 360 Designs directly to your GrabCAD Workbench or Library projects.

Supported file types

  1. Fusion 360 Design

System requirements

  1. an internet connection
  2. CPU: 64-bit processor (32-bit not supported)
  3. OS: Apple® Mac OS® X 10.7 or Microsoft® Windows® 7 or later
  4. memory: 3GB RAM (4GB recommended)
  5. hard drive space of approximately 300mb, for the Fusion 360 client
  6. a mouse or trackpad: Microsoft-compliant mouse, Apple Mouse, Magic Mouse, MacBook Pro trackpad, Space Mouse
  7. graphics card: all graphics cards on supported hardware (except Intel GMA X3100)

Description

When you have an active GrabCAD account, Autodesk Fusion 360 allows you to publish your Fusion Design directly to your Workbench or Library project.

Pricing

Free

Publish to GrabCAD comes with all versions of Autodesk Fusion 360.

How to use

  1. Install Autodesk Fusion 360
  2. Open your Fusion Design from the Fusion 360 Dashboard
  3. Click on the "Share" dropdown and select "Publish to GrabCAD"
  4. In the dialog that shows, login with your GrabCAD account
  5. Select an existing Library or Workbench project or create a new one
  6. Click "Publish here"
  7. Once the upload completes, visit your project on GrabCAD
