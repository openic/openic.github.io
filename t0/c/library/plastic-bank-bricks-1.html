My design is a "Lego" inspired "Brick"

Once the plastic has been collected and re-cycled it can be 3D printed into the desired shapes and sizes of the Brick, or standard Molds can be designed to easy the printing process.

These bricks can be used for housing/foundation and other reasons.
The brick is hollow(shelled) so that it is not to heavy.
With this idea it reduces plastic waste and opening up an opportunity for housing,reducing poverty and creating vacancies(builders).

This is just a general idea,therefor other uses of strengthening methods can be used.
