Well, this is first time I made some flying vehicle, it is only concept, I'm not sure it can fly B)
My main plan was to make comfort vehicle for four person. Another idea is 3 wheel, not four. Rear wheel-drive.

Idea was to make some elegant shape without to many parts.
And, when is in road-mode, driver have to put protection for propellers on the wings, to protect them from the garbage on the road.

And last idea, maybe you can print shell of vehicle, and printed plastic cover with carbon fiber.
