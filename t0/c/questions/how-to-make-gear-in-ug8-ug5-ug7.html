    tutorial making gear.

    1. Step 1

      - make gear folder in ug folder.
      (win7) ug folder: C:\Program Files\Siemens\NX 8.0\gear

      - attached file " gear.zip" extract in gear folder with compression program such as 7.zip.

    2. Step 2

      - start UG.
      - click 'Grip' : menu bar ' file' ->excute->grip

    3. Step 3

      - select 'gear.grx' file in gear folder.

    4. Step 4

      - select gear type. ( i selected spur)
      - click ok.

    5. Step 5

      - input gear options.
      - if the selection is done, click 'create gear'
      - click ok.

    6. Step 6

      - click yes.

    7. Step 7

      - never mind with error.
      - click terminate.

    8. Step 8

      these are gear curves.

    9. Step 9

      - extrude

    10. Step 10

      this is bevel gear modeling sample.
