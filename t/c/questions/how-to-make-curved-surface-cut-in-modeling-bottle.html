  • Frederik Vollbrecht over 1 year ago

    For round bottle:

    1. Step 1

      Create or open the main body.

    2. Step 2

      Add a sketch to right or front plane.

    3. Step 3

      Draw a rectangle. It must be vertical to the axis of rotation.
      To produce the same wavy lines on the base body, the rectangle must be constructed according to the equation e=√2 * l, where e is the corner dimension of an imaginary square and our diameter.

      Solved to l---> l=d/ √2.
      In my case l= 60mm/√2 = 42,43mm

    4. Step 4

      Add the wavelines

    5. Step 5

      Now you have to use the "project sketch" method.
      Just select the wave-sketch and the face of the rotation

    6. Step 6

      If u done it right, you have to add a plane on the end/ startpoint of the projected line and the line itself

    7. Step 7

      Create your cutting-geometrie. remember to plug the geometrie on the projected line and the outer edge.

    8. Step 8

      Cut the geometrie along the projected line.
      At this point it should look like this.

    9. Step 9

      Since the cut only goes through a quarter of the surface, it must be arranged rotationally.

    10. Step 10

      Done ;)
