    1. Step 1

      open Showcase Pro
      1-click on folder
      2-click on your file

    2. Step 2

      CATIA V5, incl. R20 (.catpart, .catproduct, .cgr)
      Cosmo scene (.csb)
      DXF (.dxf)
      DirectModel JT (.jt)
      FBX (.fbx)
      Granite (.g)
      IGES (.iges, .igs)
      NX, incl. V7/7.5 (.prt)
      Open Inventor (.iv)
      Pro/Engineer,Creo (.asm, .prt)
      STEP (.step, .stp)
      SolidWorks (.sldasm, .sldprt)
      Stereolithography (.stl, .stla, .stlb)
      2-click open

      1-click on Lighting Environements & Backgroud
