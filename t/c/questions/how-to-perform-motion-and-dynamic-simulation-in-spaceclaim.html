How to perform Motion and Dynamic Simulation in SpaceClaim?

SpaceClaim
By AR-CAD LLC on 03 Apr 02:06 0 answers 111 views 0 comments

SpaceClaim has earned a very good name as a direct modeling software and also has an easy and intuitive way to do assembly modeling. AR-CAD has developed an addin inside SpaceClaim to perform motion and dynamic simulation of a mechanism or an assembly.

A basic overview of SC-Motion 2012 (version) is given below in the form of a video tutorial at https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bLKIBMHgn1U

SC-Motion 2012 is available for 99 USD. More details at http://www.ar-cad.com/sc-motion/index.html

Other SC-Motion tutorials are at http://www.ar-cad.com/sc-motion/tutorials/index.html
