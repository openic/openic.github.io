This is my proposal for Antigravity's new PPS exterior case. This simple, sleek case is made up of two smooth, gently curved, black injection-molded plastic halves. Logos and lettering can either be projected onto the face of the shell or simple applied as a sticker. All ports, less the jump start port, are located on the side for ease of use. Alongside the ports is the power button and 10 colored power indicator lights. The front contains a large multi-LED powered light to guide you through any situation, and the light's power button. On the back side is the jump start port with a movable rubber cover that swings out of the way when in use, but remains attached to the case. A triangular cut out in the back of the case will allow for easy attachment to a lanyard. All of this was achived while staying within the design specification limits.
