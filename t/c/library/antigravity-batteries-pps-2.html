During the design I kept in mind the objective:
Ergonomic Design
Manufacturability
Mounting
Fit for Antigravity Batteries brand

At the batteries bottom there will be a narrow rubber band for the surface protection and to help maintain stability. For the current battery level there will be a five-LED indicator. The inputs and outputs are easily accessible. Captions on the cover are in the form of paints or stickers. The JUMP START port is protected with an easily removable rubber plug.
