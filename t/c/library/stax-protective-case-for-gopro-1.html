In this life there are products that are made to last forever, and one of them is the GoPro, that is why you need a strong and durable case that can support the lifestyle of this type of camera. And ... What better than a few modules 'Stax'?

But every owner is different, and one's needs can be very different from another,that's why this idea came from.

Modules to suit the needs of each user.

Module can be divided into several areas, one for the camera (green) and one for the other accessories (blue), being totally independent and having quick access to each of them. Also taking advantage of multi-colored Stax we can quickly see where this saved the desired accessory.
