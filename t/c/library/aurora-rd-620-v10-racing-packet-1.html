Aurora RD-620 V10 - Racing packet

This is only one seat car designed for fast drive on track like nurburgring. Thanks wide pushrod suspension this car can go fast through the corners and thanks big air intake and massive coolers this can be fitted with massive engines like V10 or even bigger and still be light and agile.
Its shape quarantes minimum drag and maximum downforce to make car as good as possible
Track editings includes -
1) bigger and stronger rear spoiler
2) lowered floor to minimize lift force and push air as fast as possible to back of car, channels inside the floor improves aeronamics dramaticly
3) 3 gaps in rear part generates downforce in cooperation with veins behind the car
4) and finaly cocpit glass to improve aeronamics and cover driver from dust and gravel
