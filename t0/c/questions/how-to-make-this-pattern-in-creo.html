  • Miloš Barbir 21 days ago

    If you know how to do a regular pattern this will be a piece of cake.

    1. Step 1

      Assign the direction and amount of patterns.The standard stuff.

    2. Step 2

      Then you open dimensions tab and click select dimension and you select the dimension you need.

    3. Step 3

      Then you define increment.It tells you how mush will next one be bigger than previous.

    4. Step 4

      Accept the pattern and you're done.
