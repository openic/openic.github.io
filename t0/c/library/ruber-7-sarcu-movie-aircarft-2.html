This is my design of spying drone made for movie Rubber 7.

Great aerodynamic shape allows aircraft to reach huge speed and perfect wing shaping provides enough lift to allow drone stay on for long time.
Pratt & Whitney F135 + Rolls-Royce/Allison engine gives so much power that this drone can match any jet fighter. And thanks good conception drone can also stay in air as a helicopter.
Epic agillity is given to machine by 3 sets of huge flaps. And the last one with channel inside gives unbeatable turn-radius ( no coppies allower - all rights reserved)
Because of very low weight no ammo i have fittet into this version strong hydraulics that can power big airbrake so from aircraft mode to helicopter mode time is brillianty low.
