A personal vaporizer, with soft lines and pleasant to the touch.All you are going to want to do is to hold it in your hands all the time.

Modern and aesthetically good looking design. Go ahead take it with you everywhere and Rock the vapor.

This model has a main button resembling the Logo of Rock Vapor, a USD port for recharge, a second button for adjusting settings and a LED Display.

I want to say it is production ready but it I am sure there is plenty of room for optimization, regarding clearances, selection of colors and materials, and how parts are fits together.

I used all the internal parts from Tommy Muellerâ€™s vaporizer as a reference, and everything is adjusted on the new outer shell. All parts are solids. Has a flat surface at the bottom for staying straight when is left on a surface or when recharging.
