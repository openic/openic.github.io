Customers

Manufacturing
Cameron Mitchell
Product Development Engineer

Before GrabCAD Workbench, we had unseen design revisions go to manufacturing and cause confusion and unnecessary costs. The PDM functionality in Workbench allows us to manage our CAD files and ensures every team member, both internally and externally around the world, is using the correct design revision.

Key benefit

Eliminates risk of manufacturing wrong file version

Design firm
Tony Slavik
Prototype studio manager

We have been using Workbench to communicate design iterations with our customers. Whenever we make a change or have a question about a feature, we are able to get detailed and accurate feedback without having to meet in person. This has shown some dramatic time savings as well as made for much clearer communication between us and the client. And when the customer has the power to view in 3D and add their own notes with the drop a pin feature it makes for a more informed customer which, as you can imagine, makes the entire process much smoother.

Key benefit

Improves speed and quality of client feedback

Manufacturing
Rusty Smith
Senior Engineer

We use Workbench to share files with our partners so they can easily visualize how complex assemblies should be installed and function. This saves valuable time in the process by allowing our overseas customers and suppliers to access and view the files in the browser without the need to email back and forth... which creates a bottleneck due to time zone differences.

Key benefit

Overseas suppliers respond quickly during design process

Design firm

In the world of industrial design, constant and concise communication is necessary to shorten project lead time and make sure the design of a project stays on track. Workbench helps us break down the communication barrier between the techies and the inventor of a product.

Key benefit

Shortens project timelines
