Rock Vapor challenges you to create an amazing design for the new generation of PV.

Design a cool exterior body for the next generation personal vaporizer!

Rock Vapor is a brand new, world class e-cig and vaping liquid company that has created the best tasting, highest quality and most exciting vaping brand in the world. What we need is uniquely styled PV casing that has an awesome appearance and identifies itself with the Rock Vapor brand for use with our exclusive e-liquids.

What is personal vaporizer?

A personal vaporizer (PV) is a relatively new alternative to smoking tobacco. PVs use e-liquids which are vaporized using a rechargeable battery-powered heating element and mixed within a cartomizer/clearomizer chamber. The vaporized e-liquids are then inhaled & enjoyed.

PVs are advancing in popularity and its industry is booming with a variety of functionality & features. Rock Vapor would like their PV to stand apart in crowd & have innovative appearances creating the future of cool looking PV.

Functionality & features of most personal vaporizers.

  • Uses a rechargeable battery for vaporizing
  • Has a USB rechargeable port for a long-life battery
  • Digital display showing current charge, voltage & watts, Ohms and puff counter
  • Buttons for selecting vaporizer parameters
  • Fits easily inside a shirt or pants pocket, purse, or bag
  • For safety: over-discharge & short-circuit protection, auto power-off, and 10 second cut-off during vaping.

Requirements

  • You must include:
    - STEP or IGES version
    - Multiple renderings
    - Description supporting your design

  • You are encouraged to include:
    - STL version (if your design is 3D print ready)
    - Native CAD file (SolidWorks preferred)
    - Mechanical drawings

  • Winning design will have:
    - Next Generation appearance
    - Rock Vapor branding & use of logo - download here
    - Solid description so we can understand your design better
    - Marketable & cool!

  • Design guidelines:
    - Approximate build dimensions: 170mm x 17mm x 17mm
    - Suggested wall thickness:
    ....approximately .0,14" (26 gauge) if metal
    ....approximately .060" (60 um) if plastic

If it were 2 boards: 30mm x 14mm and 10mm x 14mm is the minimum. HOWEVER, don't let these numbers get in the way of your imagination ;)
