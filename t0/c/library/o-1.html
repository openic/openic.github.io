Hello, this is my proposal: "O" - Umbra paper towel holder.
The "O" concept offer a little more height and this mean a little more comfort in use and aesthetically an interesting towel hovering sensation over the "O" ring base. This way the base is not visually obstructive and give the "air" sensation in the occupied area. The "O" ring is the base of the object, it give stability, its character and the functionality. The hole in the ring base offer the possibility to load the towel through the base leaving the upper assemblage visible joints free, this way the object will offer a clean and minimalist look. The building not require special technologies and magnetic joint between towel under and central cylinder offer a quick setup.
And since today is 8 of March this creation is a women's dedication, those who love beautiful things, and those who care for me :)
Good luck all!
