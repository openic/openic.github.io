The basic idea about this case is the usage in all envoirments: mud, snow, rain, desser (imagine your ATV won't start and you're covered in mud or you're using the lamp on the battery pack while walking home at night and its raining raining). Thats why the case is fitted with seals and seperate rubber/plastic port covers to comply with IP5x/6x standards.The case can be fitted with optional rubber feets. Available in red/yellow version.

UPDATE NOTE: Corrected spelling error on the case (Antygravity to Antigravity) and corrected alignment of the internal seal. Renamed assembly, added renderings and part files.
