Testing MoI3D for micro modelling by reverse engineering this atomising e-cigarette. Using digital callipers where possible and then modelling by eye where my callipers wont reach. More to come soon, including exploded view so you can see the detailing.

Modelling on the Atomiser is now complete, it just needs some little tweaks here and there to make it extra pretty!

The 650mAh power supply unit is now added, have some threading to model on the inner surfaces and some tidying up with very small fillets and blends, that should see the modelling complete other than branding and compliance notifications. I am not sure as to model those bits or to do them with textures.
