This concept is very easy. There's a rubber/elastic grip/push handle on the rod. Underneath the rubber there's two "bullets"(just like extension legs on tents) which you push in and that make's the lower part free. When the lower part is free it's up to you if you want to squeeze, twist or both to remove the water.
