This is my proposed design for the new socket.
I have taken the liberty to disobey the required dimensions but this is a concept design anyway. Final product would see this and other issues addressed.
Let me explain you a little bit more about this concept... It has 6 eurotype sockets, which can be turned off individually by the button on each finger (2nd finger controls two sockets) or all at once by the big button on the palm of the hand. The fingers can be closed individually to make the socket smaller or to prevent curious children from playing with the sockets and getting electrocuted. The buttons light up when the socket is on and when each individual socket is on.
The design can come in a variety of colors and combinations, which the client may choose when buying the socket online. The material would be Polypropylene with a glossy finnish. Product final cost estimations are around 20€, or 15€ without individual buttons for each socket.
Hope you like my design!
Cheers!
