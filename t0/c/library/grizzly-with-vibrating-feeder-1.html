One of the most common types of equipment used within the mining and quarrying industries. LHD / dump truck or loader discharges load onto the grizzly. Main function is to allow for finer / required sized material to fall through the grid (grizzly) onto the feeder which then discharges onto a conveyor belt. Oversized is then broken by manual labour, hydraulic rock breakers or by crushing. Definitely not the most effective way of classifying oversized from required material, but surely the most common method used throughout the world. Grizzly does not include side plates to prevent spillages
