Fun cap for watering your plants or drinks :)

Two birds with one stone. One bottle recycled without difficulty or energy loss. Second bottle 3D printed as fun bottle holder with handle. Nice thing about 3D printing is that the whole is not massive but only the walls are printed. The maximum weight requirement can so be met easy.

W.I.P.......

In 2 pictures made the holder a little transparent to look what effect it gives.
