My design of concept flying car for Terrafugia TF-X Industrial Design Challenge, smooth lines, double tail, retractable gears and led lights instead of wheels to give impression of driving through the air, are one of main characteristic of this design.
Suitable for four adults,approximate dimensions in CAR mode: Height 1.6m X Width 2m Length 4m

Its just a matter of time when we will see this crafts flying and driving around us, and I am guessing that Terrafugia will have a huge influence as one of pioneer in this industry, so don't let nothing to stop you Terrafugia.

Future is today!!!

Good luck to all!!!
