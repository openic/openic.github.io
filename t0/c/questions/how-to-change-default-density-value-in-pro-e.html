    I don't think it is possible in assembly; but you can modify material properties of a part.
    Go to File--Properties--Assign material to your part--Change the units if you want--In front of Mass Properties you will find a 'i' symbol--click on that symbol and you will find a report generated.
    In this report you can find density of a part.
