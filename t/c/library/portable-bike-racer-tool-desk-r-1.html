This is my idea for the Portable Bike Racer Tool Desk Challenge. The portable folding work desk design proposed is made out of thin sheet of aluminum. The box can be made from stamping, and the reduced number of components ensures the price remains as low as possible.
The desk has universal mounts for tools and also magnetic strips for holding parts and assorted tools, 2 spray cans of different diameters, a paper roll, and 2 drawers for tools, small spare parts and safety goggles. There is also one compartment hidden bellow the working surface for storage of bigger tools.
Four aluminum extruded standard profiles work as legs for the table, enabling outdoor use. It is also possible to easily fix it to the wall, with the provided wall clamps.
The desk assembly process is very simple and can be done in less than 5 minutes. The user has only to unscrew the 8 screws holding the legs on the bottom of the box and 4 screws holding the transversal support to the legs. Then it opens the box, pressing the two lateral pins and fixes the legs by screwing 4 screws from the inside of the box. The last part consists on fixing the transversal support to the legs with 4 screws, achieving a rigid and stable working desk. The aluminum links supporting the lid of the desk ensure that it does not accidently fall preventing injuries.
The whole box is dust and weatherproof when folded. Thanks to its slim profile, it should also be lightweight and easy to carry and store.
Hope you like my design!
Any comments/suggestions are highly appreciated!
Cheers!
