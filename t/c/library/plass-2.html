PLASS (= plastic + glass)

The basic value of a product is to fulfill a need, but this value increases if the object can communicate and educate as well.
PLASS is a glass with the very same shape of a 500 cl PET bottle's bottom. This formal analogy shows even to simplest person, like a child, that PLASS comes directly from recycled plastic bottles through a 3D printer. Besides being a useful glass, PLASS explains that collect and recycle plastic maybe useful for ourselves and for our community. Saving the environment becomes more and more interesting, valuable and colorful.
